About Goba-18

Our Heritage

We are proud alumni of Guggisberg House, Achimota Senior High School - Ghana's premier educational institution. Our brotherhood was forged in the halls of excellence.

Our Achievement

Our Brotherhood

United by shared experiences, values, and dreams. We support each other's growth, celebrate successes, and build a legacy that extends beyond school.

Our Future
